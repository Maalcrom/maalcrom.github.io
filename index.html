Brew-tiful Questions

What types of coffee do you serve?

We offer a range of coffee options, including espresso drinks like lattes and cappuccinos, brewed coffee, iced coffee, cold brew, and seasonal specialties. Decaf and plant-based milk options are also available.

Do you offer vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegan options! Our menu includes plant-based milk alternatives like almond, oat, and soy milk for any coffee or tea. We also have vegan-friendly snacks and pastries, clearly labeled for your convenience.

Can I order online?

Yes, you can order online! Simply visit Bolt Food or Glovo to place your order for pickup or delivery. Enjoy your favorite drinks and treats from the comfort of your home!

What are your opening hours?

We’re open from 9 AM to 6 PM!

Do you have loyalty programs?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program! For every purchase you make, you'll earn points that can be redeemed for discounts, free drinks, and exclusive rewards. Sign up in-store or through our app to start earning today!
